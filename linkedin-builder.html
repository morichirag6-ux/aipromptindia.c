Quick tips

1

Use OR between similar titles or industries.

2

Use AND to combine: role + industry + geography.

3

Use NOT to remove wrong profiles (student, intern, etc.).

This builder will do the formatting for you and keep everything wrapped in quotes & brackets correctly.

Your Boolean string

Fill the form on the left and click “Generate Boolean string”.

Sample: founders in SaaS / IT services in India or US

("Founder" OR "Co-founder" OR "CEO") AND ("SaaS" OR "Software" OR "IT Services") AND ("India" OR "United States" OR "USA") NOT ("intern" OR "trainee" OR "student" OR "recruiter")

Paste this into the LinkedIn search bar, then refine using filters (Location, Industry, Company headcount, etc.).

How to use the LinkedIn Builder

  1. 1

    Describe who you want

    Fill in titles, industries, geography, seniority, and exclusions (intern, student, recruiter, etc.).

  2. 2

    Generate your Boolean search

    Click “Generate Boolean string” and copy the output. The builder will create clean AND / OR / NOT logic with proper quotes and brackets.

  3. 3

    Paste into LinkedIn or Sales Navigator

    Paste into the search bar and then use standard filters (Locations, Industry, Company size, etc.) to narrow the results even further.
