Deal Doctor – Fix stuck deals with AI

Turn “stuck” deals
into clear next steps.

Instead of asking “write me a follow-up email”, give AI a full case on your deal. The Deal Doctor builder creates a diagnostic prompt that acts like a sales therapist.

  • • Works for B2B (IT services, SaaS, product) and B2C (real estate, finance, etc.).
  • • Focus on pipeline clarity: risks, stakeholders, messaging, next steps.
  • • Use together with your B2B/B2C lead builders & LinkedIn builder.

Sample AI diagnosis (summary)

**Likely problem:** You are champion-led, but the economic buyer hasn’t felt the pain strongly enough. **Risks:** - No clear “go-live” date tied to a business event. - Procurement was mentioned but not confirmed. - Your champion is over-selling internally without your help. **Next moves (7 days):** - Book a 20-minute “risk mapping” call with your champion. - Co-create a short deck for the economic buyer (3 slides). - Ask explicitly: “What happens if you do nothing for 6 months?”.

How to use Deal Doctor

  1. 1

    Describe the deal honestly

    Fill in what you sell, the company, your champion, stage, attempts, and what you think is blocking the deal. Be specific.

  2. 2

    Generate the diagnostic prompt

    Click “Generate Deal Doctor prompt” and copy it. This prompt tells AI to behave like a sales therapist, not just an email writer.

  3. 3

    Paste into ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude

    Paste the prompt, read the diagnosis, and decide what next step you’ll actually take in real life (call, email, Loom, deal review, etc.).
