The "Perfect" Prompt Formula

A "prompt" is just an instruction. To get a good answer, you need to give good instructions. Use this 4-part formula:

  1. Role: Who should the AI be? (e.g., "Act as a travel agent...")
  2. Task: What should it do? (e.g., "...plan a 3-day trip...")
  3. Context: What details does it need? (e.g., "...to Manali, for a family of 4, on a budget...")
  4. Format: How should it reply? (e.g., "...in a day-by-day table.")

Example Prompt:

"Act as an expert career coach. Write a short, professional summary for my LinkedIn profile. I am a final-year engineering student skilled in Python and Java, looking for my first job. Write it in 3 sentences."

Act Like an Expert (Personas)

If you ask a general question, you get a general answer. The fastest way to get a great answer is to tell the AI *who* to be.

This is called giving it a "persona" or "role".

Weak Prompt:

"Tell me how to write a good blog post."

Strong Prompt:

"Act as an expert SEO and content marketing specialist with 10 years of experience. Give me a step-by-step guide on how to write a blog post that will rank on Google. Include tips on keyword research, headline writing, and structure."

Prepare for a Job Interview

Use AI as your personal interview coach. You can ask it to be the interviewer and test you.

Step 1: Get Questions

"I am interviewing for a [Junior Python Developer] role at [TCS]. Act as the hiring manager and give me 10 likely technical questions and 5 behavioral questions (like 'Tell me about a time...')."

Step 2: Practice Answers

"Here is my answer to 'Tell me about a time you solved a problem': [Your answer].

Critique my answer. Tell me if it's good, and how I can make it stronger using the STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) method."

Create Stunning AI Images

Image prompts are different from text prompts. They are about *description*. The more details, the better.

Formula for Images:

(Subject) + (Action/Setting) + (Style) + (Details)

Weak Prompt:

"A picture of a woman."

Strong Prompt (Midjourney/DALL-E):

"Close-up photo of a young, happy Indian woman wearing a traditional blue sari, standing in a field of yellow mustard flowers, beautiful smile, realistic photography, golden hour lighting, high-detail, 4K"

Strong Prompt (Canva/Simpler):

"A watercolor painting of a sad robot sitting in the rain on a city street."

How to Write a Resume (CV)

AI can't *know* your experience, but it can *polish* it. The trick is to feed it your details and the job description.

The Prompt:

"Act as a professional resume writer. I am applying for a [Job Title, e.g., 'Marketing Manager'] role.

Here is the Job Description: [Paste Job Description]

Here are my bullet points of experience: - [Your old bullet point, e.g., 'I ran social media ads'] - [Your old bullet point, e.g., 'I wrote blog posts']

Rewrite my bullet points to be more professional and add metrics. Use keywords from the job description."

AI for Small Business (India)

Stop wasting time on writing. Use AI to write your product descriptions.

The Prompt:

"Act as an e-commerce expert for an Indian market. Write a compelling product description for my [Product, e.g., 'Handmade leather wallet'].

- Key Features: [e.g., '100% genuine leather', '8 card slots', 'comes in black and brown'] - Target Audience: [e.g., 'Men, professionals, as a gift'] - Tone: [e.g., 'Luxury, professional, trustworthy']

Write a 3-sentence description and 5 bullet points."

Plan a Holiday with AI

Use this prompt to get a full itinerary. The more details, the better!

The Prompt (use Gemini/Perplexity for search):

"I am planning a 3-day trip to Goa in December with my friends (we are 25 years old). We are on a budget.

- Interests: [Beaches, nightlife, cheap local food] - Don't Include: [Museums, churches]

Create a day-by-day itinerary. For each day, suggest 2-3 activities, one budget-friendly place for lunch, and one area for nightlife. Also, tell me the best way to travel (scooty, taxi, etc.)."

Create a LinkedIn Profile Summary

Your LinkedIn 'About' section is the most important part of your profile. AI can write a powerful one for you.

The Prompt (Provide Context):

"Act as a professional career coach. Write a compelling LinkedIn 'About' summary for me.

- My Role: [e.g., Final year B.Tech student] - My Skills: [e.g., Python, Java, SQL, public speaking] - My Experience: [e.g., Internship at TCS, built a 'Library Management' app] - My Goal: [e.g., Seeking a full-time 'Software Development' role]

Keep it under 150 words, write in the first person ('I am...'), and make it sound confident and professional. End with a call to action to connect."

Summarize YouTube Videos

You don't need special tools for this! Most AI chatbots (like Gemini) can't browse videos. But they *can* read transcripts.

Step 1: Get the Transcript

On YouTube, click the '...' (More) button below the video, then 'Show transcript'.

Step 2: Copy & Paste

Copy the entire transcript (it's okay if it has timestamps).

Step 3: The Prompt

"I am pasting a YouTube video transcript below. Please read it and give me:
1. A short, 1-paragraph summary of the video.
2. The 5 main key ideas or takeaways as bullet points.

[Paste the full transcript here]"

Idea Generator for Content Creators

"Creator's block" is a real problem. AI can give you endless ideas tailored to your audience.

The "Magic" Prompt:

This prompt works by setting up the AI as an expert and giving it constraints.

"Act as an expert content strategist for Indian YouTubers.

- My Channel Niche: [e.g., Budget travel in India] - My Target Audience: [e.g., College students, young professionals] - My Goal: [e.g., Get more views and subscribers]

Generate 10 viral video ideas for me. For each idea, provide a catchy, click-worthy title and a 2-sentence summary of the video's content."

You can replace 'YouTuber' with 'Blogger', 'Instagram Influencer', etc.

Write a Professional Email to a Manager

This is perfect for students or new employees who are nervous about sounding professional.

The Prompt:

"Act as a professional business communications expert.

I need to write an email to my manager, Mr. Sharma.

- Goal: [e.g., Ask for 2 days leave, from... to...] - Reason: [e.g., I have a high fever and need to rest.] - Context: [e.g., I have already informed my teammate Ravi to handle urgent tasks.]

Write a polite, formal, and short email for me. Include a subject line."

Explain Like I'm 5 (Complex Topics)

Use AI to learn *anything*. The "Explain Like I'm 5" (or ELI5) technique is a famous way to get simple answers.

The Simple Prompt:

"Explain [Quantum Computing] to me like I am 5 years old."

The Advanced Prompt (Better):

"Act as a science teacher. Explain the concept of [Blockchain] to a 10th-grade student.

- Use a simple analogy (like a school register book). - Avoid all technical jargon. - Explain *why* it is useful (e.g., security)."

How to Use AI for Code Debugging

This is one of the most powerful uses of AI for developers. It can spot errors you missed.

The Prompt:

"Act as an expert [JavaScript] developer and code debugger.

I have the following code. It is supposed to [e.g., 'make the button show an alert when clicked'] but it is not working.

[...Paste your full HTML/JS code here...]

1. Find the bug in my code. 2. Explain the bug to me in simple terms. 3. Provide the complete, corrected code block."
