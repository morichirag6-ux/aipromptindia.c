Prompt of the Day – AI Sales Missions

Train your sales brain
with a daily AI mission.

Come back every day, grab a fresh B2B or B2C mission, paste the prompt into your favourite AI, and practice turning research into real outreach.

  • • Missions for B2B (IT services, SaaS, product) and B2C (Manufacturing, Real estate, Finance, etc.).
  • • Mix of lead generation, research, copywriting, and positioning tasks.
  • • Use with your B2B/B2C builders and LinkedIn Boolean search.

Today’s mission

🏢 B2B missions

Use with B2B Lead & LinkedIn Builders.
  • Mission B2B-01 – ICP clarity

    Ask AI to create 3 clearer versions of your ICP for IT Services / SaaS – one for founders, one for CTOs, one for Heads of Ops. Each with concrete pain points and measurable outcomes.

  • Mission B2B-02 – New segment discovery

    Use AI to suggest 5 new micro-segments (by industry, tech stack, geography, company size) where your current offer would be an “easy win”.

  • Mission B2B-03 – Multi-step outreach

    Ask AI for a 3-step outbound sequence (LinkedIn DM + email + WhatsApp) tailored to one segment from your B2B Lead Builder.

  • Mission B2B-04 – Decision-maker mapping

    Pick any company list from your B2B builder and ask AI, “Who are the 3 most likely roles to own this problem inside each company?”

🛒 B2C missions

Use with B2C Lead Builder.
  • Mission B2C-01 – Brand mapping

    Ask AI to list 20 real estate / financial services / logistics brands in your city and summarise their positioning in one line each.

  • Mission B2C-02 – Consumer journey audit

    Pick 3 brands and ask AI to map their buyer journey (ad → landing page → follow-up) and highlight 3 places your offer could plug in.

  • Mission B2C-03 – WhatsApp funnel ideas

    Use AI to create a simple WhatsApp funnel (entry hooks, follow-ups, final CTA) for leads from portals or social campaigns.

  • Mission B2C-04 – “Mini campaign” pitch

    Ask AI to design a 7-day mini campaign you could propose to one B2C brand as a test project (channels, creatives, KPIs).

How to use Daily Missions

  1. 1

    Grab a mission

    Use the “Today’s mission” card or scroll B2B/B2C missions and pick one that matches your current offer.

  2. 2

    Paste into your AI tool

    Copy the mission prompt and paste into ChatGPT / Gemini / Claude / Perplexity. Use your real offer and ICP inside the prompt.

  3. 3

    Connect with your builders

    Use outputs together with the B2B/B2C lead builders and LinkedIn Boolean builder to actually find leads and start conversations.
