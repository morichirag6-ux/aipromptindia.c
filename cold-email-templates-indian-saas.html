Cold Email · B2B

Cold email templates for Indian SaaS & IT services with replies

These cold email templates are designed for Indian founders, SDRs and IT services teams selling to buyers in the US, UK and GCC. Use them to start conversations, follow up professionally and personalize outreach using AI.

1. Simple first-touch email

Subject: Quick idea for {{company_name}}


Hi {{first_name}},

I came across {{company_name}} and noticed your team is growing in {{industry}}. We help companies like yours improve {{pain_point}} through {{service_offer}}.

Would it be useful if I shared 2–3 ideas specific to your business?

Best,
{{your_name}}

2. Follow-up email

Subject: Re: Quick idea for {{company_name}}


Hi {{first_name}},

Just following up on my last email. I recorded a few quick thoughts on how {{company_name}} could improve {{pain_point}} without adding extra complexity.

Happy to send them over if useful.

Regards,
{{your_name}}

3. AI personalization prompt

Write a short cold email for an Indian IT services company targeting a {{job_title}} at {{company_name}} in {{country}}. Mention their likely business goals, one relevant pain point, and position our service as helpful but not pushy. Keep it under 120 words.

4. Best use case

  • SaaS demos
  • IT services outreach
  • Lead generation agencies
  • B2B appointment setting
  • Global outbound from India
