Sample AI output (what “good” looks like)

1) Reply to prospect (email)

Hi Priya,

Thanks again for taking the time today. I completely understand that budgets are frozen until April. To make that conversation easier when budgets reopen, I’ve attached a one-page summary of how our platform can remove manual reporting for your team and free up ~15–20 hours/month.

Would it be helpful if I also prepared a version you can share internally with finance?

Best,
Rohan

2) CRM note

30-min discovery with Priya (CTO, 200-person SaaS). Strong product fit; main blocker is budget freeze until April. Agreed to send 1-page ROI summary and finance-friendly version for internal sharing.

3) Follow-up task

“On Mon 8th April, follow up with Priya to confirm if budget discussion is scheduled. Reference ROI summary and ask if finance needs any additional numbers.”

How to use this page

  1. Select the deal stage, outcome and tone.

  3. (Optionally) add follow-up date.
  4. Click Generate AI prompt and paste it into ChatGPT / Gemini.
  5. Copy the reply, CRM note and follow-up task back into your tools.

Pro tip: Save your best prompts as a personal “Playbook” so your future teammates can use them too.
